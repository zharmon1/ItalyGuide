Overview

This is my Italy Travel Guide/Portfolio for my Creativity and Identity in Florence and Rome (ENG290/HON300) class on the KIIS Italy Summer 2023 study abroad trip. The goal is to give an overview of some of my better works and experiences on the trip. I've split these into Florence and Rome sections. I have also included my food journal from my Creativity and Identity in Florence and Rome (ENG290/HON300) class.

To the left (or above on smaller screens) is a basic map that shows every city in Italy that I visited on the trip. In order of date traveled, they are: Florence, Monterosso al Mare, Pisa, Viareggio, Fiesole, Bologna, Venice, Tivoli, Rome, and Santa Marinella

Thanks for reading, and I hope you enjoy!

-Zach Harmon
