Places Visited in Italy

Overview

This is my Italy Travel Guide/Portfolio for my Creativity and Identity in Florence and Rome (ENG290/HON300) class on the KIIS Italy Summer 2023 study abroad trip. The goal is to give an overview of some of my better works and experiences on the trip. I've split these into Florence and Rome sections. I have also included my food journal from my Italian Cuisine and Tourism (IDST350) class.

The assigned reading greatly helped shape the structure of this guide/portfolio. It includes pictures that relate to the topics, poetry over the most mundane aspects of life, analyzation of the most minute details in a work, and a guide-like narration over the city. All of these aspects of this guide/portfolio were inspired by the readings we read over the course of our stay, and they hopefully come together to make this a better piece of travel writing.

To the left (or above on smaller screens) is a basic map that shows every city in Italy that I visited on the trip. In order of date traveled, they are: Florence, Monterosso al Mare, Pisa, Viareggio, Fiesole, Bologna, Venice, Tivoli, Rome, and Santa Marinella

Thanks for reading, and I hope you enjoy!

-Zach Harmon
